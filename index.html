Plastic Pollution

Why do we need to care about it?


What is plastic pollution?


Pollution happens when we humans don't properly dispose of plastic and instead throw it in the oceans and streets . It is the accumulation of plastic objects and particles in the environment which adversely affect wildlife, wildlife habitat and humans. The plastic which enters oceans can get into the bloodstream of fishes and other sea animals. When we catch these fish to eat, it can enter our bloodstream and harm our immune system.
